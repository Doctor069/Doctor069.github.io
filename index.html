About Me

On the front-end, I have knowledge of HTML, CSS (including Flexbox and Grid), and jQuery. I am currently expanding my stack to the Node.js and React.js ecosystem, as well as deepening my knowledge of frameworks like Bootstrap to streamline the creation of responsive interfaces.

In database management, I have a solid understanding of SQL that goes beyond simple querying. My training includes data modeling (ERD), data normalization (up to 3NF), and building complex queries using JOINs, subqueries, and aggregate functions. My next step is to apply this knowledge to systems like MySQL and PostgreSQL, and to explore the NoSQL world with MongoDB and Firebase.

Among my practical projects, I developed CleikTask, a task manager for a university project, and Cleik Info, a technology e-commerce website, where I was able to apply my skills in a practical setting.

I am seeking an opportunity to collaborate on challenging projects, learn from experienced teams, and contribute my dedication and problem-solving skills.
